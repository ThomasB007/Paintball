This game is requires a Keyboard and Mouse

Controls:

In this game, you use WASD keys or Up, Down, Left and Right keys to move around. The "Up" and "Down" keys move you forward and backwards, where as the "Left" and "Right" keys are used to look around. You use your mouse to look around and aim your gun.

Aim of The Game:

The aim of this game is to shoot the enemies before the enemies kill you!
