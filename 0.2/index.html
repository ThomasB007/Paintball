(This game is requires a Keyboard and Mouse)

Controls:

Move Forward --- "W" or "Up Arrow"
Move Backward --- "S" or "Down Arrow"
Look Side to Side --- "A, D" or "Left Arrow, Right Arrow"
Reload --- "R"
Aim Gun --- Mouse
Shoot --- Press Mouse

Aim of The Game:

The aim of this game is to shoot the enemies before the enemies kill you!
